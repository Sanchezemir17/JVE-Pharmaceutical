How Does This Thing Work?

The JVE Smart Pill Dispenser works by automatically releasing the correct dose of medication at scheduled times. Users can program their pill schedule through a connected app or website, which syncs with the dispenser via Bluetooth or the cloud. When it’s time for a dose, the device dispenses the pills, alerts the user with a light or sound, and logs the intake for easy tracking and reminders.

Our Story

JVE Pharmaceutical was founded by three university students who shared a vision to improve how people manage their medications. Inspired by the challenges faced by patients in staying consistent with their treatments, they combined their skills in engineering, healthcare, and technology to design a smarter, more reliable way to take medicine. Their mission began with one simple goal — to make medication management easier, safer, and more accessible for everyone.
